The Complex Wiki

The Complex is a seperate reality from earth, entered by climing an impossibly long flight of stairs. If you do this, you will find yourself in Level 0, with no way back home. Your only option is to attempt to survive, and if you get so lucky, thrive in the new environment. This wiki is an initiative started by the Complex Reasearch and Mapping Project (C.R.M.P) to ensure wanderers are educated so they may achieve these goals.

This wiki contains four catergories. The Miscellaneous Archives are any entries that dont fit in any other categories.
