Group 0 - "The Complex Research and Mapping Project (C.R.M.P)"

DESCRIPTION

The Complex Research and Mapping Project (C.R.M.P for short) is a group dedicated to exploring, researching, mapping, and then cataloguing everything about The Complex onto a Wiki page for everyone to have access to. Although primarily a research team, it is also a fully functioning society, with bases across many levels, the most famous of which being Main Outpost, which can be found in

Level 2. The project openly welcomes new members as long as you are willing to take up a job position.

Jobs at the C.R.M.P can include exploring new levels, rescue missions, writeup work for the Wiki, entity extermination, suply runs, team managers and more. The management structure has Team managers oversaw by a layer of primary managers, who are then managed by region managers for each territory, who are appointed by five executives who are voted for democratically by all C.R.M.P members every 6 months.
