Level 9

CLASS 3 DIFFICULTY

- Occasional resources

- Complex to exit

- Entities are uncommon

DESCRIPTION

Level 9 is one of the levels in The Complex that has an outdoor setting, specifically at night. The level consists of an expansive car park outside a handful of shops, and is somewhat safe to explore. Houses and other buildings can be seen in the distance, but they are unaccessible due to the ever-expanding nature of the level. The streetlights are known to cause severe hallucinations and is the main source of insanity and even deaths in this level. Most wanderers believe that this is the safest level due to it having more resources than most levels and is easy to access from early levels, however this is far from the case. Entities are known to prey on those who have been having hallucinations for an extended period of time, as they would assume that they are merely hallucinating again - the main way to counter this is to stay looking away from the lights, you will not have hallucinations and therefore be more alert to your surroundings.

Level 9 is more plentiful in terms of resources, and you can find food, water, and other supplies within uninhabited vehicles. Some wanderers choose to use the vehicles as homes, and may get aggressive if you attempt to take their resources, so you must stay cautious. Entering Level 9 as a group is quite difficult as all wanderers start at a random location within the level.

Level 9 has an abundance of entrances and exits, and could be considered as one of the "hubs" of The Complex. However, most exits are located within the shops, which are known to be the main residences of many entities.
