Level 0

CLASS 1 DIFFICULTY

- sparse rescources

- devoid of entities

- simple to exit

DESCRIPTION

Level 0 is the first level people who enter the complex encounter. It takes the form of and infinitely expansive set of 1900s-styled stairs and hallways. The level winds constantly, making it hard to see more that a couple dozen metres ahead and navigation difficult. The level is also known to shift whilst wanderers aren't around, and shifted rooms often are lost forever, so NEVER LEAVE YOUR THINGS WHERE YOU CANT SEE THEM IN LEVEL 0. Due to Level 0 being infinitely expansive, it is virtually impossible to find other wanderers unless you enter as a group.
